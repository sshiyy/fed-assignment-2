🎶 Instructions 🎶
Your goal is simple: jump and avoid the incoming musical notes.
Use the SPACE BAR on your keyboard to make your character jump.
Timing is key! Jump too early or too late, and you'll hit a note.
As you progress, the speed and frequency of the notes will increase, testing your rhythmic skills and reflexes.
